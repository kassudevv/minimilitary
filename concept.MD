# Mini Military

Build factories to grow crops -> sell crops for money -> purchase cooler stuff. Build academies to spawn units and attack outposts. Repeat. Outposts always give active players something to do for an edge. Idle players can afk farm and get rich that way.

# Mechanics

Build factories (wheat, corn, wood, steel, oil) to earn money
Build housing (tent, house, apartment, skyscraper) to get workers

Build military academies to spawn units.
Troop mode - click on unit to select -> replaces hotbar with "troop mode". selected troops follow player. clicking on a troop again unselects. special buttons: equip all, return to base

Enemy outposts - Click on outpost with troops selected to attack. Troops will walk to outpost and battle with enemies, finally attacking the outpost and destroying it to capture.

Heroes (Special units) - Purchase dice rolls to unlock special troops like mechs, cyberpunk tanks and helicopters.
Monetization - strong heroes, strong academies

Research - Hexagonal upgrade tree to unlock the ability to buy new buildings + incremental boosts

Rebirths - Reset base progress, units and money. Keeps heroes and research progress. Unlocks rebirth units and buildings +

Arena - POST-RELEASE UPDATE IDEA Send troops to PvP arena to battle it out with other players.
